WELCOME TO RUMAH BAKTI NUR SYAHEERA

Assalammualaikum WBT

The management committee is grateful to Allah SWT, with His bounty and taufik hidayah, a home for orphans and the poor named RUMAH BAKTI NUR SYAHEERA has been successfully established. This orphanage is under the management and patronage of PERTUBUHAN KEBAJIKAN NUR SYAHEERA KUALA KUMPUR with No. registration 1379-09-WKL.

Currently, the number of orphans and poor children living in this house is 31 people and this number will be increased from time to time according to need and ability. All management, administration and expenses of these orphans are fully borne by the Nur Syaheera Welfare Organization Committee.

For now, the financial resources for this purpose are solely from donations received from generous philanthropists. The Committee warmly welcomes any form of contribution towards achieving this noble goal. May Allah SWT give taufik and His guidance to all the benefactors.

Wabillahhitaufik. Wassalam.
