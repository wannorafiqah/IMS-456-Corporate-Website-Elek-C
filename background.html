Background

Rumah Bakti Nur Syaheera was inspired by the committee to create a more perfect house to accommodate orphans and the poor. As there are many orphans and poor children in this area, a Committee was formed to achieve this goal.

After reaching a consensus, on 7th May 2009, the Committee agreed to name this organization as Nur Syaheera Welfare Organization Kuala Lumpur and name this orphanage and the poor as Rumah Bakti Nur Syaheera.This organization has received recognition from the Registrar of Societies on 1st July 2009 with No. Registration 1379-09-WKL. After that, a house was rented to house all these children. To date, a total of 24 orphans and poor boys and girls have been housed in this house. This number will be increased from time to time according to need and ability.

Establishment Objectives

  • Provide basic needs to children such as food, drink, shelter, school equipment and others according to the ability of the organization.

  • Provide adequate academic education and religious knowledge to children under the care of the organization.

  • Educate children to be useful human beings in the future and help develop themselves.

  • Train and educate children to be independent.
